Potato Bowl


Another favorite meal of mine is the potato bowl. It is quite healthy and it also tasty.

Ingredients

  • 1 Potato
  • 100 grams of egg whites
  • 1 lb of chicken
  • 1 cup of cheese of choice
  • 5 brussel sprouts

The ingredients are just a baseline but you can mix in match meats, cheese, and veggies. These are just my go-to.

Recipe

  1. Dice up potatoes, and toss in an air fryer for 20 minutes at 400°F
  2. Preheat oven to 400°F
  3. Cube and season chicken
  4. Cut brussel sprouts in half and put in oven once oven is preheated for 20 minutes
  5. Cook chicken in a pan until done
  6. In the same pan, throw in the potatoes and egg whites, and cook until egg whites are done
  7. Take concoction that was in the pan and put into bowl. Put the brussel sprouts on top
  8. Grub
