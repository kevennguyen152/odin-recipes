3 Ingredient Pasta


This pasta is a pasta I make for when I am lazy because it only involves 3 ingredients.

Ingredients

  • Pasta of your choice
  • Meat of your choice
  • Sauce of your choice

I normally choose protein farfalle, ground turkey, and just regular Ragu as my ingredients, but you can choose whatever you like.

Recipe

  1. Boil a whole box of pasta
  2. Cook your meat in a pan
  3. Once pasta is done cooking, drain it, and mix with the meat
  4. Pour sauce into meat and pasta mixture and mix
  5. Enjoy!
